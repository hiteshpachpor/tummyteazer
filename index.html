Menu

Samosa Pav

Samosa is a vegetarian fried dish with a savoury filling of spiced potatoes, cottage cheese and peas. Its size and consistency may vary, but typically it is distinctly triangular in shape. Served with a hot and sweet chutney.

Samosas are popular appetizer all over India. We proudly state that we do not use any colors or preservatives. The pastry is made in-house and fresh.

Wada Pav

Wada Pav is a vegetarian fast food dish from india. The dish consists of a deep fried potato dumpling placed inside a bread bun (pav) sliced almost in half through the middle. It is served with chutneys and a green chilli.

Originating from the streets of Mumbai, it is also called "Bombay Burger" for its resemblance to a burger.

Bhel

Bhel is made from puffed rice and Indian savory mixed with onions, tomatoes, chat masala, hot/sweet chutney and a mixture of other fried snacks as the base of the snack.

Crispy and crunchy from the puffed rice and fried savory, Bhel has a unique balance of sweet, salty, tart and spicy flavors. Topped with masala peanuts for extra crunch.

Cheese Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a fast food dish from India. Pav bhaji has many variations in ingredients and garnishes, but is essentially a spiced mixture of mashed vegetables in a thick gravy served hot with butter toasted white bread roll, usually cooked on a flat griddle.

This is topped with mozzarella cheese. We proudly state that we do not use any colors or preservatives.

Masala Chai

Masala chai is a flavored tea beverage made by brewing black tea with a mixture of aromatic Indian spices and herbs with steamed milk. Originating in the Indian subcontinent, the beverage has gained worldwide popularity.

Traditionally prepared as a decoction of green cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, ground cloves, ground ginger, and black peppercorn together with black tea leaves.

Chicken Curry

A typical curry consists of chicken stewed in an onion and tomato based sauce, flavored with ginger, garlic, tomato puree, chili peppers and a variety of spices including turmeric, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom and so on.

Usually it's served either with steamed rice or naan bread. We proudly state that we do not use any colors or preservatives.

Butter Chicken

Two simple words to describe one of the richest of Indian dishes. Succulent chicken pieces wrapped in an utterly butterly combination of cream, tomatoes and aromatic spices, this composite dish is a culinary star that tickles palates across the world.

Known as murgh makhani in Hindi, it is also one of the most popular Indian dishes recreated around the world.

Biryani

A world-renowned Indian dish, biryani takes time and practice to make but is worth every bit of the effort. Long-grained rice (like basmati) flavored with exotic spices such as saffron is layered with chicken, or vegetables cooked in a thick gravy.

The dish is then covered, its lid sealed on with dough and the biryani is cooked on a low flame. This is definitely a special occasion dish.

Shrikhand

Shrikhand is a semi soft, sweetish sour, whole milk product prepared from lactic fermented curd. The curd (dahi) is partially strained through a cloth to remove the whey and thus produce a solid mass called chakka (the basic ingredient for Shrikhand).

This chakka is mixed with the required amount of sugar, etc., to yield Shrikhand. Strands of saffron to add flavour and richness.

 

About Us

If you love authentic Indian food, Tummy Teazer is the right place for you.

We serve a wide range of authentic Indian food for veggie and meat lovers alike.

It is our belief that the food should stay true to its name without the use of preservatives or food coloring. That means we will always use fresh, 100% local ingredients in all our preparations.

We welcome one and all to Tummy Teazer to enjoy authentic Indian food. Yummy treats for tummy!

 
 

How We Started

We cook to live our dream, and it all started back in 2014 when we got a very positive feedback on our preparations in a small town called Kalgoorlie in Regional Western Australia. We quickly realised that this was our calling, and now we are making our food business dream come true through Tummy Teazer.

Although we started this new business only in 2017, it is our dedication, planning and patience of over 3 years that makes it distinctive. Not to mention years of pleasing our friends and family with lip smacking food & great taste.
